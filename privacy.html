Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At LLM Guard, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your personal data when you use our Chrome extension and visit our website.

Key Takeaway: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data when you use the LLM Guard Chrome extension. All detection and analysis happens 100% locally in your browser.

1. Data Collection from the Chrome Extension

No Data Stored or Transmitted

When you use the LLM Guard Chrome extension:

  • All sensitive data detection happens entirely within your browser
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to our servers or any third parties
  • Your browsing history, clipboard content, and pasted text remain completely private
  • Detection logs are stored locally on your device only and are never sent anywhere
  • No account or login is required to use the extension

Local Storage Only

The extension uses your browser's local storage to save your preferences and detection logs. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through the extension settings or your browser's data management tools.

2. Website Data Collection

Early Access Form

The only instance where we collect personal data is when you voluntarily fill out our early access form on this website. If you choose to sign up for early access to our enterprise features, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if provided)
  • Your company name (if provided)
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide in the form

How We Use Early Access Data

Data collected through the early access form is used exclusively for:

  • Sending you updates about LLM Guard enterprise features
  • Marketing communications related to our products
  • Providing you with early access and exclusive discounts when available

Third-Party Form Provider

Our early access form is provided by HubSpot. When you submit the form, your data is stored on HubSpot's servers. HubSpot's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, which you can review at www.hubspot.com/legal/privacy-policy.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website itself does not directly use cookies or tracking technologies. However, the HubSpot form embedded on our website may use cookies and tracking technologies to collect information about your visit and interaction with the form.

HubSpot Cookies

HubSpot may use cookies to:

  • Track form submissions and website interactions
  • Remember your information if you return to the form
  • Analyze how visitors interact with the form
  • Provide analytics to us about form performance

HubSpot's use of cookies is governed by their privacy policy. You can learn more about HubSpot's cookie policy at legal.hubspot.com/cookie-policy.

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the early access form.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. The only data we have access to is what you voluntarily provide through the early access form, and it is not shared with anyone except:

  • HubSpot (our form provider, as described above)
  • Service providers who help us with marketing communications (subject to strict confidentiality agreements)

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, but since we collect minimal data, there is very little to disclose.

5. Your Rights

If you have submitted the early access form, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

6. Data Retention

Early access form data will be retained until you request deletion or unsubscribe from our communications. We will delete your information within 30 days of such a request.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data we collect through the early access form. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Since the Chrome extension operates entirely locally, there is no data transmission risk associated with its use.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@llmguard.com

Bottom Line: The LLM Guard extension is designed with privacy at its core. We don't collect your data because we don't need it. The only data we have is what you choose to give us through our website's early access form.
